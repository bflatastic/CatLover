Cat Lovers is a content sharing website focused on telling the heartwarming stories of stray cats who have found loving homes. We provide a platform to connect stray shelters with cat lovers who are looking for a new furry friend, allowing them to portray the unique soul of each stray cat and find their perfect match.

We also offer help and advice on looking after your cat, and provide a community space where you can share photos of your beloved shelter cats and connect with other cat lovers.

Experience the life of a cat

Experience the life of cats and you will know them better, Click on the kitten to experience its life





You used to have a very warm home, and you and your owner often played together. Your favorite thing is to sleep on her shoulder. She will touch you tenderly and tell you that she will always take care of you.

However, your owner has more and more work tasks, and the time spent playing with you is gradually decreasing. When you interrupt her work to get her attention, she is no longer as gentle as before, and sometimes even yells at you. Although she would apologize to you afterwards, you know that you are getting estranged.

In order not to disturb her work, you choose to play yarn balls in the corner by yourself. One day you hear your owner call and say that she will give you to someone else. You were very sad at first, but you can understand her because she doesn't have enough time and energy to hold you. Therefore, you finally accepted her choice.

When I met my new owner, I felt that I was back to the scene where I met her (the previous owner). The bright smile of the new owner infected me, and my life was full of sunshine again.

One day, when you woke up and found yourself in a box, you slowly poked your head out and found that the surrounding environment was so strange to you. At this time you realize that you may have been abandoned by your previous owner.

Without a fixed food source, you are often hungry. You dare not go to the garbage dump and grab food from other wild cats. You can only walk by the side of the road to see if you can get away with the food leftover by others. You are very sad and fantasize that one day your master will come and find you.

In winter, you have not found a place to survive the winter, you can only sleep on the road. When the cold wind blows, you shiver. You may not be able to survive this winter quickly. At this moment you feel that someone is coming towards you. You are nervous and afraid, hoping that he is here to save you, but also afraid that he will hurt you. Your tired body makes you unable to move.

When you wake up again, you find that you are in a warm environment. The eyes of the person beside him were full of tenderness. He touched your head and told you not to be afraid, he will give you a warm home. Since then you and your new owner have lived a happy life.

When you were born, you had many brothers and sisters, and you often played carefree. As you grow up, the owner is going to give you to different people. Because a cat is pregnant, the owner considers that there are too many cats in the house and needs to give away some.

You are lucky enough to meet a good person, and she is kind to you. But it seems that she is not very good at raising cats. For example, she often forgets to prepare cat litter for you and does not know how much food should be prepared for you.

Finally, she realizes that she may not be suitable for keeping cats, so she prepares to give you to someone who is experienced in raising cats. When you heard this while sitting in a chair, you didn't feel sad, but rather happy. Because you will soon have to experience a different life in a different environment.

But the right person seems to have not come yet, and you are waiting for her with expectation and joy. Imagine the beautiful things you will experience together.
