Cat Lover is a content sharing website focused on telling the heartwarming stories of stray cats who have found loving homes. We provide a platform to connect stray shelters with cat lovers who are looking for a new furry friend, allowing them to portray the unique soul of each stray cat and find their perfect match.

We also offer help and advice on looking after your cat, and provide a community space where you can share photos of your beloved shelter cats and connect with other cat lovers.

Experience the life of a stray cat

Experience the life of stray cats and you will know them better

Scroll down to see the typical life of a stray cat.

One day, you wake up and find yourself in a box. You slowly poke your head out and find that you don't recognise where you are. You realize that you may have been abandoned by your previous owner.

Without a fixed food source, you are often hungry. You dare not go to the garbage dump and grab food from other wild cats. You can only walk by the side of the road to see if you can get away with the food leftover by others. Maybe one day your master will come and find you.

Winter comes, and you haven't found anywhere warm and safe to sleep. When the cold wind blows, you shiver. It's going to be a difficult winter. Suddenly, you feel that someone is approaching you. You are nervous and afraid, hoping that he is here to save you, but also afraid that he will hurt you. You're so tired, there's nothing you can do.

When you wake up again, you find yourself in a warm, comfortable room. The eyes of the person beside you are full of tenderness. He strokes your head and tells you not to be afraid - he will give you a warm home. Since then you and your new owner have lived a happy life.
